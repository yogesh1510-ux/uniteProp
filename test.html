ABOUT THE COMPANY

A backbone of real estate industry, scaffolding is the unsung hero that ensures safety and efficiency at every stage of construction. Scaffolding has evolved from the use of wood and bamboo to high-quality metal fittings, platforms and pipes. And with a vision to provide superior quality scaffolding with the highest safety standards, Pune Scaffolding was incepted.


Catering to some esteemed names of construction industry, Pune Scaffolding is now a respectable organization offering wide range of rental services. From undertaking turnkey projects to supply scaffolding equipment for consulting and assembling it, Pune Scaffolding ensures that it adheres to highest standards of safety, quality and service.
